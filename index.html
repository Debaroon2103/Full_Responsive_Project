About Us

Life Carriage is the Etsy of the medical world. We are a global online marketplace for people to make, sell, and buy handmade and unique medical items. Our story begins in 2022 when our founder, a medical doctor, was looking for a way to connect with other medical professionals and find unique medical supplies for his patients. He soon realized that there was no central place where doctors, nurses, and other medical professionals could buy or sell handmade and unique medical items
